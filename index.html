Yasmine Beck is an Assistant Professor of Operations Management at Eindhoven University of Technology since January 2026. From November 2024 to December 2025, she worked as a postdoctoral researcher at ESSEC Business School. Prior to this, she was a research and teaching assistant at Trier University, where she also completed her PhD in Mathematical Optimization and Operations Research. Her research focuses on mathematical modeling and the development of solution algorithms for optimization problems at the second level of the polynomial hierarchy.

Research Interests

  • Bilevel Optimization
  • Robust Optimization
  • Mixed-Integer Optimization
  • Network Pricing
  • Applications in Transportation

Education

  • PhD in Mathematical Optimization and Operations Research, 2024
    Trier University, Germany
  • Master of Science in Applied Mathematics, 2020
    Trier University, Germany
  • Bachelor of Science in Business Mathematics, 2019
    Trier University, Germany
